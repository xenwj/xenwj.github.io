用几何形状的建模图案，和转换不同的视角，形成鱼在浪潮中迁徙的视错觉。
Modeling patterns with geometric shapes and transforming different perspectives creates the optical illusion of fish migrating in waves.

         
          用同样的方式将几何体中画出鸟的图案，旁边的花纹代表太阳和月亮，代表迁徙中的鸟类披星戴月的飞翔。                   In the same way, the pattern of birds is drawn in the geometry. The pattern next to it represents the sun and the moon, and it represents the flying of migrating birds wearing stars and moons.
